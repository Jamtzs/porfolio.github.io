Kampur Cuisine A whole tradition on your plate
Kampur cuisine born with the goal of built the bridge between two cultures (Occidental and Orient) and show a whole of unique flavours in a simple plate.
  • Project
  • Kampur Cuisine
  • What I did
  • Identity, Naming
  • Project category
  • Branding, Web
  • The Challenge
  • The main objective of Kampur cuisine was to become a bridge between 2 cultures and introduce all customer in the Hindu gastronomic culture, for these reasons the design moved around different elements such as mystic items, Saa-dhu (monk), colours, and geometric patterns, etc.
  • The Solution
  • From the beginning, one of the challenges was to define those items which could able to be the key to merging the orient and occidental perception in a place and their menu. The elements that we have chosen were:
    - Saa-dhu or monk ( Holy person in Hinduism and one of the figure more know by the occidental people).
    - Geometric symbols as texture to represent of the mystic world.
    - Colour that it's from different spices used in recipes of this traditional cuisine (such as curry or turmeric).
The good brand has to print in the mind of the customer all the values of the company, just that way your client will become a branding evangelist.
