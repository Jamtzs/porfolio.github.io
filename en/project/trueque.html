The Challenge
Trueque App
The app that allows you to swap activities in these days from home

Trueque App is a social platform where the participants create a profile to offer a service in exchange for receiving another. This app allows in times of crisis to create a network of entertainment and support among the skills that each person has to offer. It is a platform to create a community of “trueque” (the spanish word for barter) / exchange of different knowledge, activities or skills. You can browse through the services that other people are willing to share and thus find the perfect “trueque”.

The Solution

The insight for this project was research understood from these pain points at the moment:
- Many people feel bored during the lockdown. They miss social contact and interacting with others.
- Parents are looking for fun activities to entertain their kids at home.
- Meeting new people gives us the inspiration to discover new things.

Problem Identify

Based on our insights that people miss social contact and interacting with others, we decided to create an app where people can to meet spontaneously, inspire and discover new things together from home.

Wireframe

After identifying the problem we moved on to wireframing. It is basically the skeleton of an app that represents the visual framework. By drawing the wireframes we were able to understand the app better and test our ideas. It also helped us to focus more on the functionality of the app.

Visual Design

Once everything else was ready, we moved on to the visual design of the app. We kept the app clean and simple to make it visually pleasing. User will be able to create a profile in truque app, using which they will do their swap of activity band meet with new people via chat and video call..
