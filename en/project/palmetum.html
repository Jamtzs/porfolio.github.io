  • The Challenge
  • Desing of iconographic for the botanical garden of Santa Cruz de Tenerife (Spain). It stands out for its geometric design and creation of contrasts supported by black tonalities. The goal is the make a contrast with the organic environment of the botanic garden and the capital of the island.
  • The Solution
  • From the beginning, the challenge was to understand, the place, the context of the use and the support where we would imprint the pictogram because the colour from the different plants could overshadow the symbols. For that reason, we have chosen the black how the background colour for the disparity with the wood and the entire colour gamut of the plants
